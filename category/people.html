People in the Fox Lab

    Andrew S. Fox

    Joined the Foxlab in July 2016

    Drew Fox is an Associate Professor in the Psychology Department at UC Davis and a Core Scientist in Neuroscience and Behavior and the California National Primate Research Center. The Fox lab is interested in understanding the neuroscience of social and emotional behavior in both humans and nonhuman primates. Fox researches modern neuroscientific approaches and computational modeling to understand emotion-related decision-making and the development of social anxiety disorder.
